Studying with notes can be an effective way to retain and understand information

1. Focus on active listening during lectures or while reading. Summarize key points in your own words rather than transcribing everything verbatim.

2. Use abbreviations, symbols, and short phrases to capture information quickly.

3. Keep your notes well-organized. Use headings, bullet points, and numbering to structure your notes logically
